Content

Home page

The home page will share the origin of how and why Jakelle's Toy Box came to be. How long it has been running and the desire to still continue this tradition. It will also have pictures of Jakelle and another of her family at the church that is and still is hosting Jakelle's Christmas Box.

Jakelles was created in the memory of JaKelle Westergard, who passed away at 17 (1994-2012). Jakelle loved both recieving and giving gifts, especially during the Holiday season. This event originally started by her family to continue Jakelle's love of giving, now has now grown into a large community event where foster families can shop for free holiday gifts for their children.

Volunteer

The volunteer page will have contact information on who to contact to set up meeting the manager to be able to volunteer at Jakelle's. Whether its just you who eants to volunteer or you're a company that wants to set up a volunteer group to help out at Jakelle's this year. It will also give a basic idea on the types of jobs that are avaliable, so if you want to help families coming through. Or help make sure toys and goodies are stocked, or to just help move boxes abround so it's easier to help stock the toys. And it'll share what week and times Jakelle's will be open this year.

Contact info Manager email: Toys@Jakelles.box

Location: 234 St N Jolly Rd2

Staring the week of Dec 8th (Monady-Friday) from 9am-4pm

At Jakelles there are different jobs you can do. You can help families shop through and make sure everyone in the family gets a gift. Don't worry carts will be provided so you don't have to carry everything. You can also choose to help set up and carry boxes of toys as they're depleted through the week. Helen's Hope Chest stockspile alot through the year so there will be many boxes in different rooms at the building. And even more boxes of toys and goodies at Helen's Hope Chest just down the street. Or you can help in the book nook and help families pick books for thier kids whether they need board book, certain reading level story, or a graphic novel. Also making sure books are stocked with different options families can choose from. But also making sure everyone who comes in has a chance to get popular books like Dr.Seuss, Dog Man, and so on.

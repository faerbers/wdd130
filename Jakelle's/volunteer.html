At Jakelles there are different jobs you can do. You can help families shop through and make sure everyone in the family gets a gift. Don't worry carts will be provided so you don't have to carry everything.

You can also choose to help set up and carry boxes of toys as they're depleted through the week. Helen's Hope Chest stockspile alot through the year so there will be many boxes in different rooms at the building. And even more boxes of toys and goodies at Helen's Hope Chest just down the street.

Or you can help in the book nook and help families pick books for thier kids whether they need board book, certain reading level story, or a graphic novel. Also making sure books are stocked with different options families can choose from. But also making sure everyone who comes in has a chance to get popular books like Dr.Seuss, Dog Man, and so on.

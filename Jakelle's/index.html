Jakelle's Toy Chest

Jakelle Westergard 1994-2012

Jakelles was created in the memory of JaKelle Westergard, who passed away at 17 (1994-2012). Jakelle loved both recieving and giving gifts, especially during the Holiday season. This event originally started by her family to continue Jakelle's love of giving, now has now grown into a large community event where foster families can shop for free holiday gifts for their children.

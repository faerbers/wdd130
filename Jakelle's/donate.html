Below is a list of types of toys and price range we like our donations to be. Also Toys that are donated for Jakelle must be brand new. Note the list below is a helpful guidline and we're open to nicer gifts as well if you have any to donate.

[Ages 3-11/ value of $20-$25 -Imaginary Play toys -Baby Dolls, Action Figures -Sports Equipment -Scooters, Trikes, & Bikes -Building Blocks or Legos -Trucks, Arts & Crafts]

[Ages 12-18/ value of $25-30 -Sports Equipment -Hair Styling Equipment, Makeup -Wireless Headphones/ Speakers -Skateboards & -Scooters -Lego's-Age 13+, Art Kits -Perfume, Colonge Fragrance Sets]

You can donate at Helen's Hope Chest at 234 St N Jolly Rd2

Or donate money directly through this QR code that we can use to buy toys in age ranges and genders we need. Or buy books we give away at the book nook at Jakelle's.
